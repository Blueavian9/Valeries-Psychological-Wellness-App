What is Holistic Therapy?

An integrative, whole-person approach to mental and physical wellness

Holistic therapy services represent an integrative, "whole-person" approach to mental and physical health that treats the mind, body, and spirit as interconnected, rather than focusing solely on symptoms. By combining traditional psychotherapy with complementary techniques like yoga, meditation, nutrition, and massage, it aims to foster balance, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about online therapy

How does online therapy work?

+

Online therapy connects you with licensed therapists via video, phone, or text. You can schedule sessions or message your therapist based on the platform's features. Most platforms offer flexible scheduling and various communication options.

Is online therapy as effective as in-person?

+

Research shows online therapy is just as effective as in-person for many conditions, including depression, anxiety, and PTSD. The key is finding a licensed therapist you connect with.

How much does online therapy cost?

+

Costs range from $35-$100+ per week or session, depending on the platform and whether you use insurance. Some platforms offer financial aid or sliding scale fees.

Do these platforms accept insurance?

+

Some platforms like Talkspace, Grow Therapy, Brightside Health, and Amwell accept certain insurance plans. Check with individual platforms for specific coverage details.

How do I choose the right platform?

+

Consider your budget, insurance coverage, preferred session type (video, text, phone), and specific needs (e.g., CBT focus, depression treatment). Use our comparison table to evaluate options side-by-side.

Are these therapists licensed?

+

Yes, all platforms listed work exclusively with licensed, credentialed mental health professionals including psychologists, therapists, and counselors.

Can I switch therapists if needed?

+

Most platforms allow you to switch therapists if you're not satisfied with your match. BetterHelp and Talkspace make this process particularly easy.

Is my information private and secure?

+

All platforms are HIPAA-compliant and use encryption to protect your information. Your therapy sessions and messages are confidential.
